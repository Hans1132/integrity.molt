Frequently asked questions

How does the free tier work?
Every visitor gets 3 Quick Scans per day, tracked by IP address. Resets at 00:00 UTC. No signup required.
What's the difference between Quick Scan and Deep Audit?
Quick Scan checks the scam database, on-chain state, and produces an IRIS risk score in about 10 seconds. Deep Audit adds holder concentration analysis, LP breakdown, deployer history, and produces a signed PDF report suitable for compliance documentation.
How are payments secured?
Card payments via Stripe. On-chain payments via x402/USDC micropayments on Solana. No credit card data is stored on our servers.
What happens if I exceed my monthly quota?
You can upgrade mid-month (prorated), or scans fall back to pay-per-scan rates (0.50 USDC Quick, 5.00 USDC Deep). You won't be cut off — no surprise bills either.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Cancellation is immediate, no questions asked. You keep access until the end of your current billing period.
Do you offer discounts?
Annual plans get 2 months free. We also offer free Protocol-tier access to public goods projects (open-source, no token, TVL >$10M). Email info@intmolt.org with details.
